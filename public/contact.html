Got Questions?

Common Questions

Q

How quickly can I expect a response after reaching out?

All inquiries submitted through the contact form are reviewed personally. You can expect a reply within 2–3 business days. For time-sensitive opportunities, please note the urgency in your message and we'll do our best to prioritise accordingly.

Q

What kinds of collaborations is Adam Rize open to?

Adam is open to a wide range of creative partnerships — from featured verses and co-written tracks to brand partnerships aligned with his music artist branding vision. Reach out with your concept and we'll explore whether it's the right fit for the project.

Q

Are press assets and media kits available for download?

Yes. High-resolution photos, official biography, and press materials are available upon request for verified media outlets, blogs, and playlist curators. Simply mention your publication or platform in your inquiry and we'll send the relevant assets directly.

Q

How do I inquire about booking Adam Rize for a live show?

For live performance and booking enquiries — including headline slots, support tours, festivals, and private events — please use the contact form and select the relevant opportunity type. Include your event date, venue capacity, and location so we can respond with accurate availability and booking details.
